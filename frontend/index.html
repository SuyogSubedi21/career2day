What is Career2Day?

Career2Day is a free career readiness platform for learners who want more than a list of tutorials. It connects role-specific career roadmaps, interview practice, quizzes, project guidance, readiness tracking, and a browser-based CV builder so every step supports the same job goal.

Start by choosing a target career such as AI Engineer, Frontend Engineer, Data Analyst, or Data Scientist. Each path shows the skills to learn, tools to practise, projects to build, and interview topics to review. As you complete work, Career2Day helps turn that proof into stronger CV sections, clearer project bullets, and better answers for recruiters and hiring managers.

What Career2Day offers

  • Step-by-step career roadmaps for technology roles.
  • Interview questions and quiz practice connected to each career path.
  • A browser-based CV builder that helps turn skills and projects into application-ready content.
  • Latest career guides about learning, portfolios, ATS keywords, interviews, and first-role preparation.
  • Weekly career tips for roadmap progress, CV improvements, interview prep, and portfolio ideas.

Featured career paths

  • AI Engineer: AI engineering is the process of building systems that can learn from data, make predictions, generate content, and automate decision-making. An AI engineer works with machine learning models, deep learning systems, data pipelines, and modern AI tools like large language models.This roadmap takes a beginner from programming basics to building and deploying real-world AI systems.
  • Machine Learning Engineer: Machine Learning Engineering focuses on building systems that can learn from data, improve over time, and make predictions or decisions in real-world applications. A Machine Learning Engineer works on data pipelines, model training, evaluation, optimization, and deployment of ML systems into production environments.This roadmap takes a beginner from programming basics to becoming job-ready in machine learning engineering.
  • Generative AI Engineer: Generative AI engineering focuses on building systems that can create content such as text, images, code, audio, and video. A Generative AI Engineer works with large language models (LLMs), diffusion models, prompt engineering, retrieval systems, and AI application development to build intelligent, production-ready AI products.This roadmap takes a beginner from programming fundamentals to building advanced generative AI applications used in real-world products.
  • AI Research Scientist: An AI Research Scientist focuses on discovering new methods, improving machine learning and deep learning models, and publishing research that pushes the boundaries of artificial intelligence. Unlike applied AI roles, this path emphasizes theory, experimentation, innovation, and scientific contribution to the field.This roadmap takes you from foundational programming to advanced AI research and paper-level understanding.
