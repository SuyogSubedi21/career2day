Enterprise Architect Roadmap

SFNS-Regular; Enterprise Architect Roadmap Enterprise Architecture focuses on designing and aligning an organizations entire technology landscape with its business goals. An Enterprise Architect works across systems, teams, and technologies to ensure scalability, governance, integration, security, and long-term strategic planning.Unlike technical roles that focus on one system, Enterprise Architecture focuses on the whole organization how all systems, data, applications, and infrastructure fit together.
