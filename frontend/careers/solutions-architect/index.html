Solutions Architect Roadmap

Solutions Architecture focuses on designing complete technical solutions that solve business problems using cloud, software, networking, and scalable system design. A Solutions Architect translates business requirements into secure, reliable, cost-efficient, and scalable system architectures.This roadmap takes you from technical fundamentals to designing enterprise-grade systems used in real-world production environments.
