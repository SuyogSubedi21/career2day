Data Product Manager Roadmap

Data Product Management focuses on building data-driven products, analytics platforms, AI-powered systems, and business intelligence solutions that help organizations make informed decisions using data. A Data Product Manager works across data engineering, analytics, machine learning, business strategy, cloud platforms, stakeholder management, and product development to transform raw data into scalable and valuable products. Unlike traditional product management roles that focus mainly on user-facing software features, Data Product Management focuses heavily on data ecosystems, analytics workflows, dashboards, AI systems, experimentation, data infrastructure, and business intelligence platforms. ⸻
