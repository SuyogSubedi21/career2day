Generative AI Engineer Roadmap

Generative AI engineering focuses on building systems that can create content such as text, images, code, audio, and video. A Generative AI Engineer works with large language models (LLMs), diffusion models, prompt engineering, retrieval systems, and AI application development to build intelligent, production-ready AI products.This roadmap takes a beginner from programming fundamentals to building advanced generative AI applications used in real-world products.
