AI Research Scientist Roadmap

An AI Research Scientist focuses on discovering new methods, improving machine learning and deep learning models, and publishing research that pushes the boundaries of artificial intelligence. Unlike applied AI roles, this path emphasizes theory, experimentation, innovation, and scientific contribution to the field.This roadmap takes you from foundational programming to advanced AI research and paper-level understanding.
