Site Reliability Engineer Roadmap

Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) Roadmap Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) focuses on building highly reliable, scalable, and efficient systems by combining software engineering with operations. An SRE ensures systems stay available, fast, and fault-tolerant while continuously improving performance through automation and observability.This roadmap takes you from foundational IT skills to managing large-scale production systems like those used by Google, Netflix, and other high-scale companies.
