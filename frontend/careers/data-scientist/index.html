Data Scientist Roadmap

Data Science is the field of extracting insights, patterns, and predictions from data using statistics, machine learning, and data analysis. A Data Scientist works with structured and unstructured data to solve business problems, build predictive models, and support decision-making.This roadmap takes a beginner from zero programming knowledge to becoming a job-ready Data Scientist.
