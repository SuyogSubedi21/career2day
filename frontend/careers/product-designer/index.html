Product Designer Roadmap

Product Design focuses on creating user-centered digital products that are visually appealing, functional, scalable, accessible, and aligned with business goals. A Product Designer works across user research, UX strategy, UI design, interaction design, product thinking, usability testing, analytics, prototyping, and cross-functional collaboration to build complete product experiences from idea to launch. Unlike traditional graphic design roles that focus mainly on visuals, Product Design combines user experience, business strategy, psychology, product thinking, and interface design to solve real-world user problems through digital products. ⸻
