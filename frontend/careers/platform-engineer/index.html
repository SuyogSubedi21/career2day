Platform Engineer Roadmap

Platform Engineering focuses on building and maintaining internal platforms that make it easier for developers to build, deploy, and scale applications. A Platform Engineer creates developer experience systems such as internal cloud platforms, CI/CD systems, infrastructure automation, and self-service tools.This roadmap takes you from foundational IT skills to designing scalable internal platforms used by engineering teams in large organizations.
