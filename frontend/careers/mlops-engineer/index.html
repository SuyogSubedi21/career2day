MLOps Engineer Roadmap

MLOps (Machine Learning Operations) is the practice of deploying, monitoring, scaling, and maintaining machine learning models in production. An MLOps Engineer ensures that ML systems are reliable, automated, reproducible, and efficient in real-world environments.This roadmap takes you from programming fundamentals to building production-grade ML systems used in real companies.
