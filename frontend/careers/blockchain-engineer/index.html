Blockchain Engineer Roadmap

Blockchain Engineering focuses on building decentralized applications, smart contracts, and distributed systems that run on blockchain networks. A Blockchain Engineer works on secure, transparent, and tamper-resistant systems used in finance, supply chain, identity, gaming, and Web3 applications.This roadmap takes you from programming basics to building real decentralized applications (dApps) and deploying smart contracts on live blockchain networks.
