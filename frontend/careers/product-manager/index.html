Product Manager Roadmap

Product Management focuses on identifying customer problems, defining product strategy, coordinating cross-functional teams, and guiding products from idea to launch and long-term growth. A Product Manager works across business strategy, user experience, software development, analytics, operations, marketing, and stakeholder communication to build successful digital products and services. Unlike software engineering roles that focus mainly on implementation, Product Management focuses on understanding customer needs, prioritizing features, aligning business goals, and ensuring that products deliver measurable value to both users and organizations. ⸻
