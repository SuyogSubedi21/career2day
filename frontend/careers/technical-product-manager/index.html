Technical Product Manager Roadmap

Technical Product Management focuses on building technology products that solve real business and user problems while coordinating engineering, business, design, operations, and stakeholders. A Technical Product Manager (TPM) works across product strategy, software systems, user experience, APIs, cloud platforms, analytics, agile workflows, and technical architecture to guide products fromideato launch and long-term growth. Unlike traditional software engineering roles that focus mainly on building systems, Technical Product Management focuses on understanding both technology and business while leading cross-functional teams to create successful digital products. ⸻
