Robotics Engineer Roadmap

Robotics Engineering focuses on designing, building, programming, and maintaining intelligent robotic systems that can interact with the physical world. A Robotics Engineer works across mechanical systems, electronics, embedded programming, sensors, computer vision, artificial intelligence, automation, control systems, and autonomous technologies to create robots for industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, aerospace, logistics, agriculture, defense, and consumer technology. Unlike traditional software engineering roles that focus mainly on digital systems, Robotics Engineering combines software, hardware, electronics, mechanics, and AI to build machines capable of sensing, decision-making, and autonomous movement in real-world environments. ⸻
